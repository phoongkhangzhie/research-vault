To answer these research questions, we followed the updated Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis (PRISMA) statement for reporting systematic reviews [12]. We screened 2,301 potentially relevant studies from eight digital libraries, considering publications between 2015 and 2024. Using inclusion and exclusion criteria along with snowball sampling, we assessed the quality of the primary studies based on their design, methodology, analysis, and conclusions. We retained a total of 81 PSs for analysis.
