Consequently, the authors should follow clear logical frameworks when organizing reviews. They can follow technical progressions, where works build upon each other sequentially to improve performance, or parallel approaches, where different papers tackle distinct tasks within a broader challenge. [...] Additionally, comparative analysis between papers reveals relative strengths, weaknesses, and applicability conditions.
