GPT-3.5 and ChatGPT achieve significant improvements over GPT-3 on the task of answering general knowledge questions. ChatGPT outperforms GPT-3.5 by over 2% in most domains. However, ChatGPT falls slightly behind GPT-3.5 on CommonsenseQA and Social IQA.
